Hidden Costs That Catch You by Surprise

The advertised price is just the beginning. Here are the costs that sneak up on you:

üí∏ Bandwidth Overage Fees

Vercel charges $40 per 100GB after your limit. A viral landing page or poorly optimized images can cost hundreds overnight. Always use next/image and monitor your bandwidth.

‚ö° Serverless Function Execution

Each API route invocation counts toward your execution time limit. Heavy compute or slow external APIs can drain your quota. Vercel charges $2 per 100 additional hours.

üî® Build Minutes

Large Next.js projects with slow CI/CD can burn through build minutes. Vercel Pro includes 6,000 mins/month, but overage costs $40 per 500 extra minutes.

üë• Per-Seat Pricing

Vercel's $20/month is PER USER. A 5-person team pays $100/month minimum. Railway and Fly.io charge by usage, not seats‚Äîhuge savings for small teams.

üóÑÔ∏è Database Costs (Separate)

No provider includes database hosting in base pricing. Budget $5-25/month for PostgreSQL on Supabase, Neon, or Railway. Vercel Storage (KV/Postgres) adds $20-100/month.

üê¢ Cold Starts on Free Tiers

Render's free tier and some serverless configs have 30-second cold starts. This kills user experience and conversion rates. Always-on costs $7-20/month but is essential for production.

Example: Real-World Monthly Cost

Scenario: 3-person team, 50K visitors/month, moderate API usage, PostgreSQL database

  • Vercel Pro: $60/month (3 users) + $10 database + $20 Vercel Postgres = $90/month
  • Railway: $15/month (usage) + $5 database = $20/month
  • Fly.io: $12/month (VMs) + $5 database = $17/month
  • Self-hosted VPS: $12/month VPS (app + database) = $12/month

Railway and Fly.io offer 70-80% cost savings compared to Vercel for small teams.
